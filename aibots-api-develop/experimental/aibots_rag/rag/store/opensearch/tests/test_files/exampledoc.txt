About OBS
 Registration/ General enquiries 

1) Where can I register for my child? 
	Please refer to the link provided in Parents Gateway for the e-Registration. Do note that SingPass login is required. 

2) When does the registration start? 
	The e-Registration period is from 16 September to 7 October 2022. 

3) Is OBS open to all Secondary 3 students or only for selected students? 
	MOE-OBS Challenge Programme is open to ALL Secondary 3 students in 2023. 

4) Will my child be required to wear a mask at all times? 
	We will adhere to the national posture at the point in time when the MOE-OBS Challenge Programme is taking place. Based on the current status, mask wearing is optional.
